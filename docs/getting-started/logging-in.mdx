# Logging In

Moda supports two authentication methods. Your administrator will tell you which one to use.

## Microsoft Entra ID (Azure AD)

If your organization uses Microsoft Entra ID:

1. Navigate to your Moda instance URL
2. Click **Sign in with Microsoft**
3. Enter your organizational credentials
4. You'll be redirected to Moda after successful authentication

## Local Authentication

If your instance uses local authentication:

1. Navigate to your Moda instance URL
2. Enter your **username** and **password**
3. Click **Sign In**

If this is your first login, you may be prompted to change your password.

## Troubleshooting

- **Account locked?** - Contact your administrator to unlock your account
- **Forgot password?** - Contact your administrator for a password reset
- **Can't access?** - Verify you have an active account with your administrator
