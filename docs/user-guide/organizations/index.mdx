# Organizations

The Organization domain manages teams, employees, and the organizational structure.

## Key Concepts

### Teams

Teams are groups of people that work together. Moda supports different team types:
- **Team** - A standard delivery team
- **Team of Teams** - A team that coordinates multiple child teams

Teams can have:
- Members (employees)
- Associated workspaces
- Planning interval participation
- Objectives and key results

### Employees

Employees represent individuals in the organization. They can be:
- Members of one or more teams
- Assignees on work items
- Synced from external directories (Microsoft Entra ID)

## Common Tasks

- Viewing and managing teams
- Managing team membership
- Viewing organizational structure
- Team of Teams hierarchy
