# Strategic Management

The Strategic Management domain supports high-level strategic planning and alignment across the organization.

## Key Concepts

### Strategic Themes

Strategic themes represent high-level organizational priorities that guide investment decisions and work prioritization.

### Alignment

Strategic management helps ensure that:
- Team objectives align with organizational strategy
- Project portfolios support strategic themes
- Work is prioritized based on strategic value

## Common Tasks

- Defining strategic themes
- Aligning objectives to strategy
- Viewing strategic alignment across teams
