# Risks

A **Risk** is the potential that a circumstance could arise that alters the outcome of a project, objective, or work item. Risks are tracked at the [team](../organizations/index.mdx#teams) level and within [Planning Intervals](./planning-intervals.mdx).

Every risk has:
- **Summary** and optional **Description**
- **Team** — Associated [team](../organizations/index.mdx#teams) (optional)
- **Reported By** — The employee who identified the risk
- **Reported On** — When the risk was identified
- **Status** — Open or Closed
- **Category** (ROAM) — How the risk is being handled:
  - **Resolved** — The risk has been eliminated; no further action needed
  - **Owned** — Someone is actively managing the risk
  - **Accepted** — The risk is acknowledged and accepted as-is
  - **Mitigated** — Actions have been taken to reduce impact or likelihood
- **Impact** — Severity if the risk materializes (Low, Medium, High)
- **Likelihood** — Probability the risk will occur (Low, Medium, High)
- **Exposure** — Automatically calculated from Impact + Likelihood:

| | Low Impact | Medium Impact | High Impact |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Low Likelihood** | Low | Low | Medium |
| **Medium Likelihood** | Low | Medium | High |
| **High Likelihood** | Medium | High | High |

- **Assignee** — Person responsible for managing the risk
- **Follow-Up Date** — When to review the risk next
- **Response** — Planned mitigation actions
- **Closed Date** — Automatically set when status changes to Closed

## Where Risks Appear

Risks are visible in multiple places:

- **[PI Risks tab](./planning-intervals.mdx)** — All risks associated with the PI's teams, with an **Include Closed Risks** toggle. Grid columns: Key, Summary, Team, Status, Category, Exposure, Follow-up Date, Assignee, Reported On.
- **[Team Risk Management tab](../organizations/index.mdx#risk-management-tab)** — Risks for a specific team, with the same toggle and create capability.
- **[Plan Review](./planning-intervals.mdx#plan-review)** — Within each team's tab during PI planning ceremonies.

## Common Tasks

### Creating a Risk

1. Navigate to a PI's Risks tab, or a team's Risk Management tab
2. Click **Create Risk**
3. Enter **Summary**, **Impact**, **Likelihood**, and **Category**
4. Optionally assign to a person and set a follow-up date

### Managing Risks

- Update **Category** as risk handling evolves (e.g., from Owned to Mitigated)
- Update **Status** to Closed when the risk is resolved — the Closed Date is set automatically
- Use the **Follow-Up Date** to schedule periodic reviews
- Use the **Include Closed Risks** toggle to review historical risks
